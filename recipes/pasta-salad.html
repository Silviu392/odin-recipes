Pasta Salad

Description

This type of pasta is one of may favorite, and I've been making it every time when I had the opportunity for a great barbecue.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta

  • 1(16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

  • 6 tablespoon salad seasoning mix

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced

  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped

  • 1 red bell pepper, diced

  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped

  • 1(2.25 ounce) can black olive, chopped

    • Steps

    1. First Step

      2. Bringing a large por of lights salted water oil. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasional, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes; rinse under cold water and drain.


    2. Second Step

      3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth


    3. Third Step

      4. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl; pour dressing over salad and toss to coat. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight.
