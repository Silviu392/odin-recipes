Bolognese Sauce

Description

A simple Bolognese recipe excellent for chunky pasta sauce with beef, pork, lots of vegetables, and tons of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of oil

  • 1 large onion, minced

  • 4 slice bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces

  • 1 clove garlic, minced

  • 1 pound lean ground beef

  • 1/2 ground pork

  • 1(28 once) can Italian plum tomatoes

  • 1/2 pound fresh mushroom, sliced

  • 6 ounces tomato sauce

  • 2 carrots, shredded

  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

  • ½ cup dry white wine

  • ½ cup chicken stock

  • ½ teaspoon dried basil

  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano

  • salt and pepper to taste

  • 1 pound pasta

Steps

  1. First Step

    2. Warm oli ina large garlic skillet over medium heat. Saute onion bacon, and garlic in hot oli until bacon is browned and crisp, about 10 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to transfer bacon mixture toa paper towel-lined palate to drain; set aside.

  2. Second Step

    3. Place the same large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef and pork in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discord grease.

  3. Third Step

    4. Stir bacon mixture, tomatoes, mushroom, tomato sauce carrots, celery, wine, stock, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper into meat mixture.Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer sauce for1 hour, string occasionally

  4. Fourth Step

    5. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook in boiling water until tooth, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.

  5. Fifth Step

    6. Serve sauce over hot pasta
